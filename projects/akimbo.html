Centennial College 2023 Fall Game Jam Winner

Overview

Over the course of a week, a dynamic team was assembled to create our jam-winning game. The theme: help make a 10-year-old's Christmas wish a reality through crafting a game with his unique monkey characters, King Ruby, Chief Plungetta and the evil skeleton monkey Skruhl.

And so started my design process, drawing both from world-building and concept art. The result, a rail-shooter based on saving the monkey kingdom from starvation caused by Skruhl's plot.

Process

This was one of my first major multi-disciplinary projects; I led other programmers through version control protocol.

Coding-wise, I was in charge of te following mechanics:

• UI functionality
Menu Navigation, Settings, Healthbar, and Energy Bar.

• Turret enemy coding
I completed a coding brief of creating a stationary enemy with 4 states.

State Name Description
IDLE
  • Enemy remains stationary
  • Plays the idle animation.
  • No awareness of PLAYER
TRACKING
  • Enemy remains stationary
  • Plays the "open" animation. Where the orb is exposed.
  • Has awareness of PLAYER
  • Waits a certain amount of time with the player being in the tracking area to move to the Wind Up phase.
WIND UP
  • Enemy remains stationary
  • Plays the "WindUp" animation. That alters core colour for a period before moving to the ATTACK state.
  • Awareness of PLAYER
ATTACK
  • Enemy remains stationary
  • Fires projectile(s) at player directly at player. (I later upgraded it to a multi-pattern system)
  • Returns to WIND UP State after attacking.

• Player pick ups
Created a variable health and energy pick up system with a loot table for enemies to streamline designer use of systems.
Additionally, created a "Key" (battery) drop. Which I then made open doors as detailed by the design team.

• Oversaw dialogue system integration
Supervised usage of the Fungus Add On, coded in dialogue transitions and consulted narrative designer for more detailed implemenation.
