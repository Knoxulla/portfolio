Overview

Archie is a newly-born archling in a world he once found familiar. Help guide him through the Avalonian ruins to help find his lost memories. However, roaming these halls old sentinels of the Avalonian people: Cariburns. These machines, now corrupted by a mysterious energy, have turned their sights onto their own creators. But don't be afraid to fight, for if you get hurt, there will always have someone by your side. Farewell Avalon is a top-down combat game with a unique twist to dying and a deep storyline in a world unlike before.
