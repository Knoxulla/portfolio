Overview

Farewell Avalon is a top-down combat game with a unique twist to dying and a deep storyline in a world unlike before.


Archie is a newly born archling in a world he once found familiar.
Help guide him through the Avalonian ruins to help find his lost memories. However, roaming these halls are old sentinels of the Avalonian people: Cariburns. These machines, now corrupted by a mysterious energy, have turned their sights onto their own creators. But don't be afraid to fight, for if you get hurt, there will always be someone by your side.
